Blueberry Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 1 medium banana (approximately 6 inches long), sliced and frozen
  • 1/2 cup frozen blueberries (not thawed)
  • 1 container (8 ounces) nonfat, artificially sweetened vanilla yogurt
  • 1/4 cup skim milk

Instructions

  1. Step 1:
    Place ice cubes at the bottom of the blender.
  2. Step 2:
    Add banana slices, blueberries, yogurt, and milk. Cover and blend in pulses until smooth, stopping frequently to stir down the ice.
  3. Step 2:
    Serve right away. Freeze leftovers in an airtight, microwave-safe container and thaw in the microwave until slushy.

Banana Oats Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 1/2 cup of oats
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup of milk of choice
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tsp of butter or oil to spray pan
  • Brown sugar

Instructions

  1. Step 1:
    Pulse oats in a blender for 5-10 seconds then place in large bowl.
  2. Step 2:
    In a small bowl, mash banana with a fork until mixed in a semi-creamy texture. Place to the side.
  3. Step 3:
    Add vanilla extract, egg, brown sugar, salt, and milk in a separate bowl. Add the combined mixture to the mashed banana in the large bowl. Stir well.
  4. Step 4:
    Preheat skillet to medium-high heat.
  5. Step 5:
    Scoop out banana oat mixture in the skillet. Cook each side until you see the edges turn brown.
